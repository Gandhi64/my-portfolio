Compact Reconfigurable Antenna For 5g Band

The demand for compact sized antennas is increasing due to the rapid expansion of technologies like IoT. Micro strip patch antennas are used in IoT applications due to their compatibility, low profile, lightweight and precision applications in the microwave frequency region. This work is designing a compact configurable antenna for 5G band. Three types of antenna were designed: rectangular microstrip antenna, H shaped antenna and hexagonal shaped antenna. The performance of each antenna was analyzed to determine their suitability for various applications. The development of advanced antennas is essential for improving the performance and requirements of wireless communication systems.
